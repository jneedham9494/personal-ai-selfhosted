### Part 6: Decide Whether to Continue (10 min)

**This is THE moment your Type 3 pattern tries to quit**

**Project:** [[Reading/Combating-Competitive-Quitting-Pattern#Weekly-Evaluation-Framework]]

Ask yourself honestly:

- [ ] Do I want to quit any goal right now?
  - Which one(s)?
  - Why? (be specific)

**If YES to quitting:**

1. **STOP** - This is exactly when the system matters most
2. Check hours invested in that goal: ___/100
3. If <100 hours: **You cannot quit yet** (Pre-Commitment Contract)
4. Re-read your contract: [[Projects/[GOAL]/Pre-Commitment-Contract]]
5. Use 72-Hour Rule: Mark calendar for Thu to reconsider
6. Ask: "Am I quitting because I'm bad, or because I'm in bottom half?"
7. If bottom half anxiety: This is THE pattern. Don't feed it.
8. Decision: Continue Week 2? (Y/N) - Must choose Continue if <100h

**If NO to quitting:**

Great! But stay alert - quit-urge typically hits Week 2-4 when novelty fades.
