**Project:** [[Projects/Fitness/Health-and-Fitness-Goals]]

**Plan:** [[Week-1-Meal-Plan]]

**Why meal prep:** Prevents weekend decision fatigue, supports fitness goals, saves time/money

**Friday Evening Tasks (After workout):**

- [ ] Review [[Week-1-Meal-Plan#Shopping-List]] (@2025-11-14 18:00)
  - Check pantry for items you already have
  - Print/screenshot shopping list
  - Adjust quantities if needed

- [ ] Grocery shopping (@2025-11-14 18:30)
  - Go to preferred store
  - Follow Week 1 Shopping List (organized by section)
  - Estimated cost: $80-100 for the week
  - Stock up on proteins, produce, grains

- [ ] Batch Cook Main Meals (@2025-11-14 19:30)

  **Batch 1: Turkey Chili (makes 6 servings)**
  - Sauté onion + bell pepper + garlic (5 min)
  - Brown 2 lbs ground turkey (8 min)
  - Add tomatoes, beans, broth, spices (5 min)
  - Simmer 30 min (do Batch 2 during this time)

  **Batch 2: Grilled Chicken (makes 5 servings)**
  - Season 3 lbs chicken breasts
  - Grill or bake at 400°F for 20-25 min
  - Let rest, then slice

  **Batch 3: Brown Rice (makes 5 cups)**
  - Use rice cooker or stovetop (45 min)
  - Cook while doing other batches

  **Batch 4: Steamed Broccoli (for 5 bowls)**
  - Steam 5 cups broccoli florets (8 min)

- [ ] Portion & Store (@2025-11-14 21:00)
  - Assemble 5 Chicken & Rice Bowls (chicken + rice + broccoli)
  - Portion 4 Turkey Chili containers (1.5 cups each)
  - Label each container with day (Mon-Thu)
  - Store in fridge

**Completion Checklist:**
- [ ] Turkey Chili cooked and portioned (4 servings)
- [ ] Chicken breasts grilled and sliced (5 servings)
- [ ] Brown rice cooked (5 cups)
- [ ] Broccoli steamed (5 cups)
- [ ] 5 Chicken & Rice Bowls assembled
- [ ] All containers labeled

**Mindset Reminder:**
- This is about FUNCTION not PERFECTION
- Week 1 meals don't need to look Instagram-worthy
- Type 3 will want aesthetic meal prep photos → resist, stay in Stealth Mode
- Simple, protein-rich meals = fuel for muscle building
