## Obstacles & Solutions

**Obstacle: "I'm bored without Twitter/YouTube"**
- **Solution:** Have a list of 10 alternatives ready (see "Alternatives to Phone Scrolling" section)
- **Reframe:** Boredom is the space where creativity happens

**Obstacle: "I need Twitter for news/work"**
- **Solution:** Check Twitter desktop ONCE daily, 30 min max, in the afternoon
- **Better:** Use RSS feeds, newsletters, or news apps for updates

**Obstacle: "What if I miss something important?"**
- **Solution:** If it's truly important, you'll hear about it another way
- **Reality check:** Did you miss anything important while traveling? Probably not.

**Obstacle: "My friends message me on social media"**
- **Solution:** Tell them to text/call you instead (share your number)
- **Use:** WhatsApp/Signal for important conversations (Screen Time exempt)

**Obstacle: "Work requires me to be online constantly"**
- **Solution:** Set work hours boundaries (9am-6pm), then phone locks
- **Communicate:** Let team know you're less available evenings/weekends

**Obstacle: "I'll reinstall the apps when stressed"**
- **Solution:** Delete apps PLUS remove accounts from phone (requires re-login)
- **Friction:** Makes reinstalling annoying enough to avoid
