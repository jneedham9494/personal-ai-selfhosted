# Weekly Meal Plan

## Nutrition Overview

**Daily Targets:**
- Calories: 2,400-2,800
- Protein: 160-180g
- Carbs: 250-300g (higher for swimming/cycling)
- Fats: 70-90g
- Water: 4+ liters

**Key Points:**
- Pre-swim: Banana + honey (30-60 min before)
- Post-workout: Protein shake + electrolytes within 30 min
- Post-sauna: Water with LMNT packet
- Keep protein bars/almonds at work for cycling commute

- Drink 16-20oz water with LMNT packet immediately after

## 📖 Recipes

### Breakfast Recipes

**Scrambled Eggs with Avocado**
- 3 eggs, whisked
- 1/2 avocado, sliced
- Salt, pepper
- Heat pan, scramble eggs 2-3 min, serve with avocado

**Protein Oatmeal**
- 1/2 cup oats
- 1 cup water or milk
- 1 scoop protein powder (mix after cooking)
- Berries, almond butter
- Microwave 2 min, stir in protein, top with berries

**Protein Pancakes**
- TJ's buttermilk pancake mix
- Add 1 scoop protein powder to batter
- Cook on griddle, flip when bubbles form
- Top with berries and maple syrup

---

### Lunch Recipes

**Grilled Chicken Breast**
- 6-8 oz chicken breast
- Everything But The Bagel seasoning
- Olive oil
- Grill 6-7 min per side (internal temp 165°F)

**Turkey Burgers**
- 6 oz ground turkey
- Salt, pepper, garlic powder
- Form patty, grill 5-6 min per side
- Serve with sweet potato or rice

**Chicken Caesar Salad**
- Grilled chicken (pre-cooked), sliced
- Spinach
- Caesar dressing (TJ's)
- Feta cheese
- Toss and serve with whole wheat tortilla

---

### Dinner Recipes

**Baked Salmon with Sweet Potato**
- 6 oz salmon fillet
- 1 large sweet potato
- Olive oil, salt, pepper, lemon
- Bake salmon 400°F for 12-15 min
- Roast sweet potato 425°F for 25-30 min

**Grilled Chicken with Rice**
- 6-8 oz chicken breast
- Soyaki sauce (marinade 30 min)
- 1.5 cups jasmine rice (cooked)
- Roasted broccoli
- Grill 6-7 min per side

**Turkey Meatball Spaghetti**
- 1 lb ground turkey
- 1 egg, breadcrumbs, garlic, salt, pepper
- Form meatballs, bake 400°F for 20 min
- Cook whole wheat pasta
- Heat TJ's marinara sauce, combine

**Easy Stir-Fry**
- Protein of choice (chicken, turkey), cubed
- Frozen stir-fry vegetables (TJ's)
- Soyaki sauce
- Cook protein, add vegetables, add sauce
- Serve over jasmine rice

### Meal Prep Recipes

**Bulk Grilled Chicken (Sunday Prep)**
- 3-4 lbs chicken breast
- Everything But The Bagel seasoning
- Olive oil
- Season all chicken, grill 6-7 min per side
- Let cool, portion into containers
- Stores 4-5 days in fridge

**Jasmine Rice (Bulk)**
- 2 cups uncooked rice
- 4 cups water
- Bring to boil, reduce heat, simmer 15 min
- Makes ~5 cups cooked
- Portion into containers

**Roasted Vegetables**
- Broccoli, peppers, onions (chopped)
- Olive oil, salt, pepper
- Roast 425°F for 20-25 min
- Portion for week

**Hard Boiled Eggs (Batch)**
- 12 eggs
- Boil water, add eggs, cook 10 min
- Ice bath, peel when cooled
- Store in fridge (eat within 7 days)
