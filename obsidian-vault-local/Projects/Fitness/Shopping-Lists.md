## 📦 Amazon (Supplements)

**Essential Supplements**

**1. Protein Powder**
- Optimum Nutrition Gold Standard Whey (5 lbs, ~$60)
- Dosage: 1-2 scoops daily
- Flavors: Double Rich Chocolate, Vanilla

**2. Creatine**
- Optimum Nutrition Micronized Creatine (~$20)
- Dosage: 5g daily (1 tsp)

**3. Electrolytes**
- LMNT Electrolyte Drink Mix (~$45 for 30 packets)
- Use: 1 packet post-swim or post-sauna
- Alternative: Liquid IV (~$25 for 16 sticks)

**4. Fish Oil (Omega-3)**
- Nordic Naturals Ultimate Omega (~$35)
- OR Costco/Kirkland Omega-3 (better price)
- Dosage: 2 softgels daily

**5. Vitamin D3 + K2**
- Sports Research Vitamin D3+K2 (~$20)
- Dosage: 1 softgel daily (5000 IU)
