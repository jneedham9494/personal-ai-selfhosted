### **Monday: PUSH DAY (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps) + Sauna**

**Best Times:**
- Morning: 6:00-7:30am (before work)
- Evening: 7:30-9:00pm (after work, avoid 4-7pm peak)

**Workout (45-60 min):**

**Chest:**
- Barbell bench press: 4x8-10 (heavy)
- Incline dumbbell press: 3x10-12
- Dumbbell chest flies: 3x12-15

**Shoulders:**
- Overhead press (barbell or dumbbell): 4x8-10
- Lateral raises: 3x12-15
- Front raises: 3x12-15

**Triceps:**
- Tricep dips: 3x8-12
- Cable tricep pushdowns: 3x12-15
- Overhead tricep extension: 3x12

**Post-Workout:**
- Sauna: 15-20 minutes
- Protein shake + electrolytes
- Stretch

### **Tuesday: SWIM + Sauna**

**Best Times:**
- Morning: 6:00-7:30am (empty pool!)
- Late evening: 8:00-9:30pm (after peak)

**Workout (45-60 min):**
- Warm-up: 200 yards easy (4 laps)
- Main Set:
  - 10x50 yards freestyle (moderate, 15 sec rest)
  - 4x100 yards (mix strokes: free, back, breast)
  - 4x25 yards sprint (all-out)
  - 200 yards cooldown
- **Total:** 1,200-1,500 yards

**Post-Swim:**
- Sauna: 15-20 minutes
- Hydrate with electrolytes immediately
- Protein shake with carbs

**Nutrition:**
- Pre-swim: Banana + honey (60 min before)
- Post-swim: Protein + carbs within 30 min

## Strength Progression Plan

**Start Week 1-2 (Build baseline):**
- Learn form on all movements
- Use lighter weights
- Focus on technique
- Full range of motion

**Week 3-4 (Increase volume):**
- Add weight gradually (5-10 lbs per week)
- Track all lifts
- Aim for progression

**Week 5-8 (Build strength):**
- Progressive overload
- Track PRs (personal records)
- Challenge yourself safely

## Obstacles & Solutions

**Common Obstacles:**
- "Too busy" → Schedule it like a meeting
- "Too tired" → Start with 10 minutes
- "Don't feel like it" → Do it anyway (discipline)
- "Travel/disruption" → Bodyweight workouts anywhere
- "Injury" → Modify, don't stop completely

**Backup Plans:**
- Bad weather: Home workout
- No time: Quick 15-min HIIT
- Travel: Hotel room workout
- Sick: Rest (it's okay!)
