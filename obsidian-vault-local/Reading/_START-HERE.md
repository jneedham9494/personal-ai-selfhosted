## 🚨 When Quit-Urge Hits (Emergency Protocol)

**Step 1:** STOP. Do NOT decide to quit right now.

**Step 2:** Use the 72-Hour Rule
- Wait 72 hours before making any quit decision
- Re-read your Pre-Commitment Contract for that goal
- Check 100-Hour Tracker: If < 100 hours, you're NOT ALLOWED to quit

**Step 3:** Journal the urge
- What triggered this? (comparison? pain? boredom?)
- Which enneagram pattern is this? (Type 3 image management? Type 7 boredom?)
- Am I in Week 3-4? (expected quit-danger zone)

**Step 4:** Use the mantra
> "I am loved and worthy, even when I fail."

**Step 5:** Message accountability partner or use Telegram bot
- "Quit-urge hitting for [goal]. Need pep talk."

**99% of time, urge passes by Day 3.**
