## 🚨 Emergency Protocols

### When Quit-Urge Hits

**Step 1: STOP (Do not decide now)**
- Close this tracker
- Do NOT quit for 72 hours
- Mark quit-urge above

**Step 2: Document (within 1 hour)**
- Which goal: ____________
- What triggered it: ____________
- Am I in Week 3-4? (danger zone)
- Hours invested so far: ___/100

**Step 3: Re-read Contract (Day 1)**
- Open Pre-Commitment Contract for this goal
- Read out loud
- Remember why you started

**Step 4: Accountability (Day 2)**
- Text accountability partner
- Or message Telegram bot with Claude
- Script: "Quit-urge hitting for [goal], help me reality-check"

**Step 5: Decision (Day 3)**
- If still want to quit: Is this quitting or pivoting?
- Quitting = comparison/shame (DON'T DO IT)
- Pivoting = genuine misalignment (rare, but valid)
