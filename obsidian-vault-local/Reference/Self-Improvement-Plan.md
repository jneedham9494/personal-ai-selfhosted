# Self-Improvement Plan - Type 3 (The Achiever)

> *"I am loved and I am worthy, even when I fail."*

---

## 📊 My Profile Summary

**Primary Type**: Type 3 - The Achiever (98% match)
**Wings**: Type 2 (74%), Type 4 (61%)
**Arrows**: Type 6 (54%), Type 9 (55%)

### Top 3 Superpowers
1. **Ambition** (19/20) - Goal-oriented, makes big things happen
2. **Analysis** (19/20) - Deep thinker, thoughtful decision-maker
3. **Assertiveness** (18/20) - Clear about goals, asks for what I want

### Top 3 Blind Spots
1. **Image** (20/20) ⚠️ - Overly concerned with appearances over substance
2. **Superiority** (16/20) - Pressure to be "the best," can come off arrogant
3. **Pain Avoidance** (15/20) - Disconnects from difficult emotions

---

## 🎯 Core Growth Areas

### 1. Authenticity Over Image
**The Challenge**: I cultivate my image carefully, sometimes prioritizing how things look over how they feel.

**Action Steps**:
- [ ] **Daily Authenticity Check**: Ask "Am I doing this for me or for how it looks?"
- [ ] **Weekly Vulnerability Practice**: Share one "imperfect" thing with someone I trust
- [ ] **Relationship Audit**: Identify 2-3 people who know the "real" me
  - Person 1: _______________
  - Person 2: _______________
  - Person 3: _______________
- [ ] **Social Media Detox**: 30-day break from comparison platforms
- [ ] **Imperfection Practice**: Do something imperfectly on purpose this week

**Success Metric**: Can I be authentic even when it doesn't look impressive?

---

### 2. Humility & Connection Over Superiority
**The Challenge**: I believe I should be better than others, creating pressure and distance.

**Action Steps**:
- [ ] **Daily Gratitude**: Write how others have helped me succeed
- [ ] **Weekly Help Request**: Ask someone for assistance (practice vulnerability)
- [ ] **Daily Celebration**: Genuinely praise someone else's achievement
- [ ] **Failure Sharing**: Share a recent mistake/struggle with someone safe
- [ ] **Mentorship**: Find 1 person to learn from (not compete with)
  - Mentor: _______________
  - What I want to learn: _______________

**Success Metric**: Do I celebrate others' wins without comparing myself?

---

### 3. Feeling Emotions vs. Avoiding Pain
**The Challenge**: I disconnect from difficult emotions and stay in "doing" mode.

**Action Steps**:
- [ ] **Emotion Check-ins**: Set 3 daily alarms to pause and notice feelings
  - Morning: 9:00 AM
  - Afternoon: 2:00 PM
  - Evening: 7:00 PM
- [ ] **Sit With Discomfort**: When sad/anxious, resist fixing it with productivity (5 min minimum)
- [ ] **Feeling Journal**: Daily entry on what I'm actually feeling (not just what I did)
- [ ] **Therapy/Coaching**: Research and book first session
  - Therapist options: _______________
  - First session date: _______________
- [ ] **Unstructured Time**: 2 hours weekly with no agenda, no achievement

**Success Metric**: Can I sit with sadness for 5 minutes without distracting myself?

## ⚡ Quick Reference

### When I'm Performing (Not Being Real):
1. Pause
2. Read the mantra
3. Ask: "What am I really feeling?"
4. Share something authentic with someone safe

### When I'm Avoiding Feelings:
1. Notice the urge to "do"
2. Sit for 5 minutes without distraction
3. Name the emotion
4. Remind myself: sadness will pass, and that's okay

### When I'm Being Superior:
1. Notice the judgment
2. Ask: "What am I insecure about?"
3. Practice gratitude for someone who helped me
4. Reach out for help with something

### When I Care Too Much About Image:
1. Ask: "Do I want this or does it just look good?"
2. Share something imperfect
3. Spend time with someone who knows the real me
4. Make a choice based on values, not optics
